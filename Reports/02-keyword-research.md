## Keyword Strategy Summary

Modgill Travels operates in a high-intent, location-dependent niche where searchers typically have immediate booking intent — they need a cab now or for a planned trip in the next few days. The primary opportunity is to dominate transactional local searches for Patiala-origin routes (especially Patiala to Delhi, Shimla, Manali, and Amritsar), while building topical authority across all Punjab corridor routes. A secondary tier targets travelers coming from Delhi, Chandigarh, and Amritsar who need return-leg cabs back into Punjab. The GEO/AEO opportunity is strong: queries like "best taxi from Patiala to Delhi" and "how much does a cab from Patiala to Manali cost" are being answered by AI Overviews, making structured FAQ and price-transparency content critical for citation capture.
